# BRAND

## Brand Decision

- Service name: `다밋`
- English wordmark: `DAMIT`
- Recommended product slug: `damit`
- Recommended Supabase staging project: `damit-staging`
- Recommended future production DB project: `damit-prod`

## PM Judgment

PM decision: add light CI now.

Why:

- the product is moving from internal prototype to a more credible beta surface
- users should feel they are entering a product, not a raw internal tool
- a basic CI system improves trust before heavy marketing spend is relevant

What to add now:

- wordmark
- simple symbol mark
- primary color direction
- one-line brand promise

What not to overbuild yet:

- full brand book
- expensive illustration system
- complex motion identity
- large-scale marketing site copy

## Brand Position

`다밋` means helping messy field conversations land in a cleaner ending.

The brand should feel:

- calm
- competent
- modern
- trustworthy
- not aggressive or overly "AI" styled

## Brand Promise

- Korean: `현장 설명을 더 매끄럽게 끝내는 기록 도구`
- Short version: `설명은 빠르게, 기록은 분명하게`
- English helper line: `Make field changes easier to explain and easier to trust.`

## Tone

- practical over flashy
- reassuring over clever
- service-minded over technical

## Visual Direction

- deep navy base for trust
- blue for clarity and workflow confidence
- warm amber point color for human warmth and action
- rounded shapes with compact spacing

## Current rollout scope

- landing page
- login page
- beta home entry surface
- product planning docs

## Deferred rollout

- in-app full visual refresh
- domain rename
- deployed Fly app rename
- production email sender rename
