# DAMIT_BRAND_UI_SYSTEM

## PM goal

Build a product-wide UI foundation that feels unmistakably like `다밋`, not a generic AI SaaS shell.

## Design thesis

`다밋` should look like a modern field-operations desk:

- clear enough for fast work
- warm enough for owner trust
- structured enough for evidence and audit

The product should not feel like:

- an AI playground
- a glass dashboard
- a “future-tech” landing page

## Visual metaphor

- field ledger
- evidence folder
- stamped status labels
- clipped paper cards
- operations desk

## Foundation tokens

### Backgrounds

- warm paper base instead of cold white-blue gradients
- subtle ruled texture or document-like structure
- low-gloss surfaces, almost no blur

### Cards

- solid paper cards
- visible borders
- sharper geometry than the current soft-glass style
- one stronger top-level card per section for primary focus

### Emphasis

- blue = action confidence
- amber = caution or human follow-up
- green = closure or stable proof

### Labels

- small uppercase labels act like docket or ledger markers
- badges should read like status stamps, not decorative chips

## Surface roles

### Landing

- product credibility
- calm promise
- one strong brand moment, not many decorative moments

### Login / Start

- trusted entry
- obvious next step
- no “magic” feel; the flow should feel operational and reassuring

### Home

- routing desk
- current company + next action
- clear split between primary route and supporting routes

### App

- main operating workspace
- most task-dense surface
- every section should feel like part of one evidence workflow

### Ops

- operating console
- highest-risk item must stand out immediately
- “what to open next” should feel like dispatch, not dashboard trivia

### Account

- owner control surface
- security and invitations first, history second

### Admin

- internal-only
- constrained, quieter, more audit-like than product marketing-like

## Anti-patterns to remove

- floating frosted cards everywhere
- shiny bright blue hero gradients
- overly soft rounded blobs
- decorative emphasis that competes with the first action
- pages that look visually unrelated even when functionally connected

## Phase 1 implementation

1. redefine shared color and surface tokens
2. remove most blur/glass treatment
3. strengthen card borders and hierarchy
4. unify shell styling across landing, auth, workspace, and ops
5. update the brand mark to match the product system
6. verify all authenticated visual-review surfaces still feel coherent

## Phase 1 success criteria

- landing, login, home, app, and ops feel like one product family
- screenshots no longer read as generic AI SaaS
- the product feels calmer, more grounded, and more operational
